SolarEX Quartz

SolarEX Quartz — SiO₂

Quartz is positioned as a transparent SiO₂ nanocoating that creates hydrophobic and oleophobic surface behavior, helping water, dust, and other contaminants release more easily from the panel surface. This is the passive self-cleaning path within the SolarEX portfolio.

  • 100–150 nm coating thickness positioning
  • 5–15 mL/m² application range
  • Anti-stick, easy-clean, and durability narrative
  • Field evidence from the Hochdorf monitored rooftop case

SolarEX Titan

SolarEX Titan — TiO₂

Titan is positioned as a photocatalytic TiO₂ nanocoating that actively decomposes organic contamination under light exposure while also creating superhydrophilic rinsing behavior. This is the active self-cleaning path within the SolarEX portfolio.

  • Study-based 5.15% average yield improvement narrative
  • 360-day monitored PV³ study structure
  • Superhydrophilic water sheeting behavior
  • String-level results and revenue implication block

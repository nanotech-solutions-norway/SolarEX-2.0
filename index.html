SolarEX Quartz

SolarEX Quartz — SiO₂

Quartz is positioned as a transparent SiO₂ nanocoating that creates hydrophobic and oleophobic surface behavior, helping water, dust, and other contaminants release from the panel surface more easily. This is the passive self-cleaning path within the SolarEX portfolio.

  • 100–150 nm coating thickness positioning
  • 5–15 mL/m² application range
  • Anti-stick, easy-clean, and durability narrative
  • Europe and Middle East ROI pathways

SolarEX Titan

SolarEX Titan — TiO₂

Titan is positioned as a photocatalytic TiO₂ nanocoating that actively decomposes organic contamination under light exposure while also creating superhydrophilic rinsing behavior. This is the active self-cleaning path within the SolarEX portfolio.

  • Study-based 5.15% yield improvement narrative
  • 360-day monitored field-study framing
  • Superhydrophilic water sheeting behavior
  • Organic contamination handling advantage

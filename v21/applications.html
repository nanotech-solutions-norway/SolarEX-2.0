Application fit and qualification logic

Choose the route that matches the real fouling mechanism.

SolarEX v2.1 is mechanism-led. The correct recommendation depends on whether dust and mineral spotting dominate, or whether organic contamination and UV-activated rinsing behavior matter more for the site.

Open QuartzOpen Titan

Quartz is strongest where

  • Dust-heavy utility and C&I sites dominate
  • Desert or semi-arid abrasive particulate loads are common
  • Hydrophobic release and lower wash burden are the key value drivers
  • Passive self-cleaning logic is more relevant than photocatalysis

Titan is strongest where

  • Repeated pollen, bird fouling, algae, or leaf-film contamination occurs
  • UV exposure supports active self-cleaning behavior
  • Superhydrophilic rinse consistency is valuable
  • Operators need monitored-performance evidence for review

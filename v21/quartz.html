SolarEX Quartz | SiO₂ nanocoating

Hydrophobic easy-clean protection for photovoltaic glass.

SolarEX Quartz is the passive self-cleaning route in the SolarEX platform. It is designed for low adhesion, easy rinsing, and lower dust or mineral-spot persistence on solar glass where the commercial case is tied to wash burden, transparency retention, and stable operating output.

Technical profile

  • Passive low-adhesion easy-clean layer
  • Hydrophobic and oleophobic surface behavior
  • pH stability framing: 1.5–12.5
  • Ethanol-rich solvent system with flammable-liquid classification
  • HVLP spray, wipe, cloth, or sponge application routes in current material

Commercial profile

  • Quartz Europe payback framing: 147 days
  • Quartz Middle East payback framing: 218 days
  • Europe annual gain framing: ~€6.07/m²
  • Europe 5-year profit framing: ~€27.92/m² before maintenance savings

Where Quartz is strongest

  • Dust-heavy utility and C&I sites
  • Sand exposure and mineral spotting
  • Sites where wash interval and labor or water burden are material
  • Locations where passive self-cleaning logic is more relevant than photocatalysis

Deployment constraints

  • Do not apply on damp surfaces or in humid conditions
  • Avoid direct sunlight during application
  • Do not apply when panel surface exceeds 30°C
  • Use a controlled surface-preparation and alcohol-clean sequence before laydown

What a good Quartz pilot should prove

  • Lower visible soiling persistence
  • Longer acceptable wash interval
  • Better treated-vs-control output under the same environment
  • Commercial value that includes both energy and O&M benefit

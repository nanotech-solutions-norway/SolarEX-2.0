SolarEX Quartz | SiO₂ nanocoating

Hydrophobic easy-clean protection for photovoltaic glass.

SolarEX Quartz is the passive self-cleaning route in the SolarEX portfolio. The coating creates an ultrathin transparent SiO₂ layer that lowers contaminant adhesion, supports easy rinsing, and helps preserve optical performance by slowing soiling build-up on solar glass.

Why Quartz is positioned for dust, sand, and wash-reduction logic

Quartz is strongest where low surface adhesion and retained transparency matter more than photocatalytic organic breakdown. It is therefore a fit for dusty utility fields, mineral spotting, abrasive particulate exposure, and cleaning regimes where water or labor costs are material decision variables.

Technical frame

  • Coating thickness: 100–150 nm
  • Coverage framing: 5–15 mL/m²
  • Application modes: HVLP spray or lint-free cloth
  • pH stability framing: 1.5–12.5
  • Full cure in approximately 24 hours
  • Optically clear and effectively invisible on the surface

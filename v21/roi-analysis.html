How to interpret the numbers

  • Quartz Europe and Middle East are primarily commercial scenario models.
  • Titan Europe carries a stronger monitored-uplift basis because it uses the rooftop study as its anchor.
  • Titan high-insolation remains a scenario model even when it inherits the monitored uplift basis.
  • Cleaning-labor and water savings can materially improve the total case beyond the electricity-value model alone.

Quartz Europe model chain

  • 90 W/m² × 2,335 sunshine hours = 210.15 kWh/m²/year baseline
  • 10% uplift assumption → ~21 kWh/m²/year gain
  • €0.289/kWh → ~€6.07/m² annual gain framing
  • Quartz coating cost assumption → 147-day payback framing

Titan study-to-value chain

  • Measured uplift basis: +5.15%
  • 63 coated modules / 360 days / 15-minute monitoring
  • Per coated string revenue increase in study: €128.94/year
  • Full-system extrapolation in study report: ~€24,627/year

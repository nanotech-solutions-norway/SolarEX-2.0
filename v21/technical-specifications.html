Technical specifications

Current technical framing from the SolarEX source set.

This page consolidates the current technical parameters used across Quartz and Titan materials so reviewers can interpret mechanism, dosage, cure time, and fit with fewer document jumps.

Quartz | SiO₂

  • Layer thickness: 100–150 nm
  • Application method: spray or wipe
  • Coverage framing: 5–15 mL/m²
  • Full cure: 24 hours
  • pH stability: 1.5–12.5
  • Mechanism: hydrophobic and oleophobic low-adhesion easy-clean layer

Titan | TiO₂

  • Nanostructured TiO₂ route
  • Application method: spray or wipe
  • Coverage framing: 5–15 mL/m²
  • Full cure: 24 hours
  • Mechanism: photocatalysis plus superhydrophilic rinse action
  • Best fit: organic fouling and UV-activated rinse logic

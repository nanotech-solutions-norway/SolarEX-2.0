Application process

From site intake to treated-versus-control pilot review.

The cleanest SolarEX workflow is intake, mechanism match, treated-versus-control pilot design, and then measured commercial interpretation using yield plus O&M effects.

1. Intake

  • Site location and climate
  • Panel type and age
  • Fouling profile
  • Current cleaning routine

2. Match

  • Quartz for dust and spotting
  • Titan for organic contamination
  • Define evaluation period
  • Confirm success metrics

3. Validate

  • Treated-versus-control setup
  • Output tracking
  • Wash and labor tracking
  • ROI review

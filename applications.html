Application fit

Select the coating mechanism based on the contamination profile, not on generic assumptions.

SolarEX Quartz is the passive hydrophobic easy-clean pathway. SolarEX Titan is the active photocatalytic daylight-cleaning pathway. The correct product depends on what is actually happening on the site.

Site profileRecommended pathReasoning
Dust, spotting, general outdoor soilingSolarEX QuartzPassive hydrophobic and easy-clean behavior is usually the strongest fit
Organic fouling, pollen, microbes, bird contaminationSolarEX TitanPhotocatalytic decomposition under sunlight creates more differentiated value
High water-use and cleaning-cost pressureQuartz or TitanThe mechanism should follow the fouling type, not a generic preference
Mixed-fouling solar fieldsPilot comparisonRun a structured field pilot to validate the higher-value mechanism

SolarEX Titan

SolarEX Titan is the TiO₂ product pathway in the SolarEX portfolio. It is positioned around photocatalytic action under light exposure, active breakdown of organic contamination, and superhydrophilic water sheeting for more uniform rinsing.

Titan positioning summary

  • Photocatalytic self-cleaning under UV exposure
  • Superhydrophilic surface behavior after activation
  • Study-based average yield improvement narrative of 5.15%
  • Long-duration proof story built around a 360-day monitored study

How Titan works

The Titan story is built around TiO₂ nanoparticles that become active under light exposure. In site-level commercial language, this supports continuous breakdown of organic residues while also creating a water-sheeting surface that helps rinse away loosened contamination more evenly.

Why Titan is strategically different

Where Quartz emphasizes passive anti-stick behavior, Titan emphasizes active cleaning chemistry. That makes the product separation commercially useful rather than cosmetic, especially for markets with persistent pollen, bird fouling, algae, biofilm, or other organic contamination pressures.

SolarEX Titan

SolarEX Titan is the TiO₂ product pathway in the SolarEX portfolio. It is positioned around photocatalytic action under light exposure, active breakdown of organic contamination, and superhydrophilic water sheeting for more uniform rinsing. How Titan works

The Titan story is built around TiO₂ nanoparticles that become active under light exposure. In site-level commercial language, this supports continuous breakdown of organic residues while also creating a water-sheeting surface that helps rinse away loosened contamination more evenly.

Why Titan is strategically different

Where Quartz emphasises passive anti-stick behaviour, Titan emphasises active cleaning chemistry. That makes the product separation commercially useful rather than cosmetic, especially for markets with persistent pollen, bird fouling, algae, biofilm, or other organic contamination pressures.

Why the PV³ study matters

The uploaded Titan study is more rigorous than a standard brochure claim. Measurement data was captured at 15-minute intervals through Smartblue Smart Control and the work was supervised by a publicly appointed and sworn photovoltaic expert.

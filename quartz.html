SolarEX Quartz | SiO₂ nanocoating

Hydrophobic easy-clean protection for photovoltaic glass.

SolarEX Quartz is the passive self-cleaning pathway in the SolarEX portfolio. The coating forms an ultra-thin transparent silica nano-film that reduces adhesion of water, dust, oily residues, and general environmental contamination, helping maintain cleaner active surfaces with lower maintenance burden.

How Quartz works

Silica nanostructure with low-adhesion surface behavior

SolarEX Quartz is based on SiO₂ nanoparticles in a glass-like matrix. The resulting transparent nanostructure reduces surface energy and supports beading-and-roll-off behavior, so rain, dew, or light cleaning can remove contamination more effectively than on untreated glass.

The source material repeatedly frames Quartz around three operational outcomes: high transparency, hydrophobic and oleophobic repellency, and durable easy-clean performance.

Why choose Quartz

Best fit when passive easy-clean behavior is the priority

  • Utility and commercial solar where dust, spotting, and general outdoor contamination dominate
  • Sites where lower cleaning frequency and lower water demand have direct economic value
  • Projects that require transparent protection with limited impact on visual light transmission
  • Operating profiles where robust durability and broad chemical resistance matter
  • Hot-climate use cases where water-based variants can support more uniform deposition

Application guidance

Representative implementation path

1. Surface preparation

Clean the panel surface thoroughly and remove residues before application.

2. Thin uniform deposition

Apply by spray or wipe using the reported low-volume coverage range.

3. Dry and cure

Allow rapid initial drying and a representative full-cure period of around 24 hours.

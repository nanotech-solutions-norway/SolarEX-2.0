### Quartz / SiO₂
- hydrophobic and oleophobic behavior
- anti-stick and easy-clean framing
- 100–150 nm coating thickness
- 5–15 mL/m² application range
- pH stability in acidic and alkaline environments
- ROI examples for Europe and Middle East
- warm-climate formulation note: water-based version suitable where slower evaporation improves uniformity

### Titan / TiO₂
- photocatalytic active cleaning under UV exposure
- superhydrophilic water sheeting
- 360-day monitored field-study narrative
- average 5.15% energy gain in source study material
- panel string and plant-scale revenue framing in study material
- pH stability and thin transparent layer positioning

### Shared themes

Study methodology

The system comprised several groups of arrays (GAKs), each containing six strings of 21 identical modules. In three separate GAKs — GAK 4, GAK 8, and GAK 14 — one string was coated with SolarEX Titan while the remaining five strings in each GAK remained uncoated. Performance data was recorded through Smartblue Smart Control at 15-minute intervals.

The coating was applied on 13 October 2022 according to the recommended application procedure. Data collection ran from 8 February 2023 to 3 February 2024. The installation was regularly serviced, including biannual cleaning, and the study implementation was supervised and documented by Thilo Scharf, a publicly appointed and sworn expert in photovoltaic system technology.

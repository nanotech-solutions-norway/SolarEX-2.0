Technology overview

Two nanocoating pathways. Two different cleaning logics.

The SolarEX website is built around a crucial distinction: Quartz uses a passive SiO₂ anti-stick / easy-clean mechanism, while Titan uses an active TiO₂ photocatalytic mechanism with superhydrophilic rinsing behaviour. This page now makes that distinction more visual and easier to evaluate.

Quartz technology logic

Quartz is built on SiO₂ nanostructure logic that supports reduced contaminant adhesion, easier natural cleaning, and maintenance reduction. The uploaded material also supports durability, transparency, and climate-specific formulation discussion.

Titan technology logic

Titan is built on TiO₂ nanostructure logic where light exposure drives reactive cleaning behaviour. The uploaded Titan material further supports UV-related functionality, superhydrophilic rinsing, and a stronger long-study proof architecture.

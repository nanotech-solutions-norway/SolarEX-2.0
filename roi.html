ROI drivers

  • Local energy price per kWh
  • Solar irradiance / annual operating profile
  • Soiling burden and contamination type
  • Cleaning frequency and water cost profile
  • Coating cost per square meter

Commercial message discipline

The site should frame ROI as scenario-based and evidence-supported, not as a blanket guarantee. That improves trustworthiness and reduces commercial risk while still retaining a strong business case narrative.

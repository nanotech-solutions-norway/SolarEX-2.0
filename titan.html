SolarEX Titan | TiO₂ nanocoating

Active self-cleaning through photocatalysis and superhydrophilic rinsing

SolarEX Titan is formulated around TiO₂ nanoparticles. Under UV light, the coating becomes active, helping break down organic contamination while also supporting uniform water sheeting for cleaner surfaces after rainfall.

Mechanism

How SolarEX Titan works

01

Light activation

UV and blue-spectrum light excite electrons in TiO₂ particles, activating the coating.

02

Radical generation

Reactive oxygen species are produced and begin attacking organic contaminants on the surface.

03

Water sheeting rinse

After activation, water spreads in a thin film rather than discrete droplets, improving rinsing uniformity.

Why choose Titan

Strong fit where organic fouling is operationally significant

Titan is particularly attractive where passive hydrophobic behavior alone is not enough. If the solar environment includes organic films, pollen, microbial activity, bird fouling, or similar contamination, TiO₂'s active mechanism provides a differentiated value proposition.

The uploaded material also positions Titan as relevant where UV-screening and air-purification narratives are commercially valuable.
